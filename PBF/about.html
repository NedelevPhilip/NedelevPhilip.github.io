About Us

PB Fulfilment was established as Paton Brown in 1960 and has been packing, fulfilling and sending out mail and parcels on behalf of our clients to their customers for 60 years.

Our main aim is to provide a cost effective and liberating warehousing and fulfilment operation to our clients that saves them money and time. We take care of all your products logistics from managing goods in, stock management, picking and packing your orders, shipping and eturns. We can even go one step further and take on all your customer service enquiries.

We offer a bespoke, scalable service that works just as efficiently whether you are a global brand or a sole trader. Our solutions are tailored to address special client requirements and we pride ourselves in our flexibility to meet client’s needs.
